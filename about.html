Who We Are

Your Trusted IT Partner in Pune

Vigilant IT Solutions was founded with a simple mission: to provide reliable, professional IT support that businesses can count on. Based in Pune, Maharashtra, we've been serving individuals, small businesses, and enterprises since 2015.

We understand that technology should enable your business, not hinder it. That's why we focus on preventive maintenance, quick response support, and solutions that are tailored to your specific needs and budget.

Our team of certified IT professionals brings together expertise in hardware, networking, cybersecurity, and enterprise infrastructure. We're not just service providers – we're partners invested in your success.
